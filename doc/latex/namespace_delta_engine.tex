\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A 2d engine made with \href{sfml-dev.org}{\texttt{ SFML}} (window,render,sound) and \href{box2d.ord}{\texttt{ Box2D}} (physics). 

\hypertarget{namespace_delta_engine_autotoc_md0}{}\doxysubsubsection{Delta\+Engine}\label{namespace_delta_engine_autotoc_md0}
It uses multiples classes, firstly \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_game}{Game}}, that contains infos about your project (name, version, debug/release mode, etc...) and your \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_object}{Objects}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_entity}{Entities}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_light}{Lights}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_shader_manager}{Shaders}}. This class draws everything on the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_game_a61e86878be90b835fd10e84a151a322e}{window}} too.

\mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_object}{Objects}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_entity}{Entities}} are pretty the same, but \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_entity}{Entities}} can move and \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_object}{Objects}} are statics. They\textquotesingle{}re containers of \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_part}{Parts}}.

\mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_part}{Parts}} are a combination of a texture (\href{sfml-dev.org}{\texttt{ SFML}}) and an hitbox (\href{box2d.ord}{\texttt{ Box2D}}). They can be animated and \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_shader_manager}{Shaders}} can be applied on.

\mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_light}{Lights}} enlight the scene. They can be circular or directional and you can change their \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_light_a40d9df2c826f7ce0ea20d6cfb015a524}{color}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_light_aab425e8b17a3380a6f229f862ed11b91}{brightness}}.

\mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_shader_manager}{Shader\+Managers}} manage and stock all your \mbox{\hyperlink{class_delta_engine_1_1_shader_manager}{Shaders}}. They\textquotesingle{}re accessible by their index.
